How We Think

Principles, not process

01

Production first

Anyone can wire up a dev board. We design for the factory from day one — DFM analysis, thermal simulation, EMI/EMC compliance, and component sourcing baked into every schematic. The prototype is the product.

02

Let AI do the grunt work

Trace routing, component placement, design rule checks — machines are better at this than humans. We're building AI tools that compress weeks of layout work into hours, so engineers focus on what actually matters: architecture.

03

Own the full stack

A PCB is useless without firmware. Firmware is useless without connectivity. Connectivity is useless without a cloud backend. We deliver complete embedded systems — sensor to cloud, edge to dashboard, device to fleet management.

04

Solve real problems

Wearable monitors catching cardiac events in rural clinics. LoRa sensors cutting water waste on farms. Radar tracking vital signs without touching skin. We choose projects where hardware changes outcomes, not just aesthetics.

Capabilities

What we build

Full-spectrum embedded hardware development. No handoffs, no gaps, no excuses.

Custom PCB Design

Multi-layer layouts optimized for signal integrity, thermal performance, and manufacturability. AI-assisted placement and routing cuts turnaround from weeks to days. We deliver production-ready Gerbers, not just pretty schematics.

Embedded Firmware

STM32, ESP32, nRF52, and custom SoCs. RTOS, bare-metal, or embedded Linux — whatever the application demands. BLE, Wi-Fi, LoRa, LTE-M, NB-IoT. Code that survives in the field, not just the lab.

IoT Product Development

Complete device-to-cloud architecture. Sensor integration, gateway design, OTA update systems, real-time monitoring dashboards, and fleet management. Industrial-grade reliability meets consumer-grade simplicity.

Medical Devices

Remote patient monitoring, wearable vital-sign sensors, and radar-based contactless health tracking. Designed for regulatory compliance (CE, BIS) and real-world deployment in hospitals and rural clinics alike.

Asset Tracking

GPS + LoRa + cellular trackers for logistics, agriculture, and fleet management. Ultra-low-power designs that run for years on a single charge. Built for Indian summers and Himalayan winters.

Edge AI Systems

On-device machine learning for computer vision, anomaly detection, spectral sensing, and predictive maintenance. Intelligence that runs at the edge — no cloud dependency, no latency, no compromise.
