Philosophy

Innovation x Vision

01

Design with intelligence

Every PCB layout, every component choice, every trace route — informed by AI. We don't replace the engineer, we amplify them. Machine learning handles the tedious; humans handle the creative.

02

Ship real products

Prototypes are easy. Production is where most startups fail. We bridge that gap — DFM analysis, thermal simulation, compliance-ready outputs. Your hardware works in the lab and in the field.

03

Think in systems

A board is nothing without firmware, connectivity, and a deployment strategy. We architect complete embedded systems — sensor to cloud, edge to dashboard, device to fleet.

04

Build for impact

Healthcare monitors saving lives in rural India. Precision agriculture sensors cutting water waste. Asset trackers protecting supply chains. Technology exists to solve real problems.

What We Do

The work

End-to-end hardware development — every discipline under one roof.

PCB Design

AI-accelerated schematic capture, multi-layer layout, signal integrity analysis, and production-ready Gerber files. From concept to fab in record time.

Embedded Systems

Firmware development for STM32, ESP32, nRF, and custom SoCs. RTOS, bare-metal, and Linux. BLE, Wi-Fi, LoRa, cellular connectivity.

IoT Solutions

Sensor networks, cloud integration, OTA updates, fleet management dashboards. Complete device-to-cloud architecture for industrial and consumer IoT.

Health Monitoring

Remote patient monitoring devices, wearable sensors, radar-based vitals detection. Medical-grade hardware designed for real-world deployment.

Asset Tracking

GPS + LoRa + cellular tracking devices for logistics, agriculture, and fleet management. Low-power, long-range, built for harsh environments.

AI + Hardware

Computer vision quality control, edge ML inference, spectral sensing, and predictive maintenance systems. Intelligence where it matters — on the device.
